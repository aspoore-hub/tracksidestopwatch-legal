Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 3, 2026

Trackside Stopwatch is designed for coaches and athletes to run and manage timing sessions. We respect your privacy.

Information We Collect

Trackside Stopwatch stores timer history, lap splits, roster names entered by the user, app settings/preferences, and subscription status provided by Apple locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to external servers controlled by the developer.

Purchases & Subscriptions

Premium purchases are processed through Apple’s App Store. We do not collect or store payment information. Apple may provide subscription status information required to unlock features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information or share user data with third parties.
